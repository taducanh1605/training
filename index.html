User manual:


Two modes of training:
- You can click on banner to change between 2 modes.
- At the first mode, you can select an existing exercise program.
- At the second mode, you can load an exercise program from CSV file.

How to practice:
1. Click "Start" button to start the workout.
2. After finish the exercise, click "Done" button to take a break.
3. You can pause, skip or backward the rounds if you need.

For each exercise:
- The exercises are placed in the list, you can click on them to find the movement instructions.
- The x10 symbol indicates that you must do 10 reps for that move.
- The symbol x10-5-5 indicates that you have to do 10 reps, then hold that move for 5 seconds, and finally do 5 more reps for that move.
- The symbol x30s indicates that you have to hold that move for 30 seconds

For more training programs:
- You can contact to me to have more training programs. Then I can design a workout program for you.
